What is FCFS Scheduling

The FCFS algorithm is regarded as the most basic operating system scheduling mechanism. According to the first-come, first-serve scheduling technique, which uses a FIFO queue, the process that demands the CPU first gets it first. FCFS is a non-preemptive scheduling algorithm.

The advantages of FCFS are easy to implement and it is in a first come, first serve method. The disadvantages of FCFS are it suffers from Convoy effect, the average waiting time is much higher than the other algorithms and it is very simple and easy to implement and hence not much efficient.
